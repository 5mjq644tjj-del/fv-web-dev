Privacy & GDPR — FV Web Development

This page explains how FV Web Development collects and handles personal data. It is a basic, compliant starting point for local business websites. This is not legal advice. Adapt and review with a lawyer for full compliance.

Data collection

Contact requests submitted via the website are sent to the email address hello@fvwebdev.com. The form collects: business name, contact name, phone number, and message. This data is used only to respond to your request.

Retention

Personal data submitted is retained until the request is closed, then kept only for bookkeeping or legal purposes for up to 24 months unless otherwise agreed.

Third parties

The site uses third-party services for hosting and (optionally) analytics. No tracking scripts are included by default on this template. If analytics are added, disclose them here and provide opt-out instructions.

Rights

Data subjects can request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing. Contact hello@fvwebdev.com to exercise rights.

Contact

For privacy questions contact: hello@fvwebdev.com
